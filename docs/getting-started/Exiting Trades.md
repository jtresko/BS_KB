Even though finding a good trade is the thing traders love to talk about most, it's at least as important to nail the exit. BigShort makes it easy for traders time their exits to maximize profit and avoid a painful turn.   
  
Here we walk through several examples of recent SPY activity and how you could have used the basic BigShort indicators to have ample time to make clean exits.   
  
If you prefer, [page through the presentation on your own](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1L_-LryBLVEF-cwj9stv4OPTIxsaStTWPnJM0-2dgwlI/edit?usp=sharing).
