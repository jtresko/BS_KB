DarkFlow is a discovery tool that provides a ranked list of tickers with the most significant current Dark Pool activity. It helps traders identify where large off-exchange trades are happening across the market, rather than focusing on a specific ticker.

![](/assets/images/help/getting-started/DarkFlow Tab_BigShort-01-23-2025_02_54_PM.png)

To best understand this tab and how to make use of it in your trading strategy, we recommend reading our complete article about [dark pools and DarkFlow.](/docs/indicators-and-features/Understanding Dark Pools and DarkFlow in BigShort/)
