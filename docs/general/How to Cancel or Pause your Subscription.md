If you'd like to pause or cancel your subscription, sign into app.bigshort.com, then click on the down caret in the upper right-hand corner, and select "Customer Portal."

![](/assets/images/help/general/How to Cancel or Pau_customer+portal+highlighted.png)

You'll be redirected to our Stripe Portal. From here, select the **Cancel subscription** button and hit "Confirm." If you change your mind, you can always renew your subscription.
