To change your payment method, sign into app.bigshort.com, then click on the down caret in the upper right-hand corner, and select "Customer Portal."

![](/assets/images/help/general/How to Change your P_customer+portal+highlighted.png)

You'll be redirected to our Stripe Portal. From here, select "Add payment method" under **Payment Method**. You'll have a confirmation code sent to your contact phone number, be sure to enter that in. Your option are credit card, bank, or Google Pay. Enter your new payment method, and hit **Add**.
