To change your billing address, sign into app.bigshort.com, then click on the down caret in the upper right-hand corner, and select "Customer Portal."

![](/assets/images/help/general/How to Change your B_customer+portal+highlighted.png)

You'll be redirected to our Stripe Portal. Under**Billing Information** , select "Update information". From here, you can update your name, address, and phone number. Then hit "Save", and you're all set!
