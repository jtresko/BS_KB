# Step 1

Join the BigShort Discord server [here](https://discord.com/invite/6w22gAVzMB). Be sure to join with the same Discord username you want to access the members-only channels with, we allow one username per account.

# Step 2

Sign into app.bigshort.com, then click on the down caret in the upper right-hand corner, and select "Members Discord."

![](/assets/images/help/discord-community/How to Join our Memb_customer+portal+members+discord.png)

Then follow the prompts. Once you've completed the prompts, you should be able to see these additional channels:

![](/assets/images/help/discord-community/How to Join our Memb_subscriber-only+channels.png)

Please stick to the topic of each channel! We discuss charts and trades in real-time here, so we highly recommend joining. The #official-tae-recs channel is where Tae makes his trading recommendations, currently the channel boasts a 100% win rate.
