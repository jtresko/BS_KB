# The BigFour BigShort Indicators

You will often see BigShort traders talk about the "BigFour" or main BigShort indicators. While BigShort offers dozens of different datapoints to help you trade, the BigFour are critical to learn and master.

## The BigFour

### 1. Dark Pools

Off-market trades that reveal where institutional money is positioning. Dark pool prints on the chart show support and resistance levels.

## Using the BigFour Together

The key to successful trading with BigShort is looking for **confluence** - when multiple indicators align in the same direction. 

**Bullish confluence example:**
- Dark pools clustered below current price (support)
- NOF trending positive (bullish options flow)
- MomoFlow shadow bars pointing down (momo selling, MMs buying)
- SmartFlow positive (smart money accumulating)

When all four indicators align, you have a high-probability setup. When they conflict, exercise caution.
