On the [dashboard tab](https://help.bigshort.com/en/articles/10362240-dashboard-top-10s) (pink box, above) you'll find:

  * Left - Top 10 NOFA - the cumulative measurement of [Net Options Flow](https://help.bigshort.com/en/articles/10354751-net-option-flow-a-complete-guide) activity

  * Center - Top 10 Positive [MomoFlow or Smartflow](https://help.bigshort.com/en/articles/10361455-understanding-smartflow-and-momoflow-indicators) \- toggle between the two on the top right of the page by clicking the gear (blue box, above)

  * Right - Top 10 SE - stocks with the highest short exempt volume




These show you real time insight into what tickers have heavy activity - these are great trading ideas. To dive into each ticker, just click and you'll be taken to the [SF Segregated](https://help.bigshort.com/en/articles/10362301-sf-segregated-tab) tab with that ticker loaded up, ready to analyze and trade if the signals support it.

# Second Step - Check the OptionFlow tab

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/u9e1zc58/1387562201/12d2444901e4e4611889e19190cf/OptionFlow.png?expires=1765215000&signature=8e3bb8445c3b6d33fb73371ad7a3b6c08e5587fe9a1900eff56129d7bf8adf0b&req=dSMvEcx4n4NfWPMW1HO4ze25539ph6Zx9F5jtwYqnsTuZAe3cFPdiEd1RRjm%0A11Tl9CBkEP4r36iW79o%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/u9e1zc58/1387562201/12d2444901e4e4611889e19190cf/OptionFlow.png?expires=1765215000&signature=8e3bb8445c3b6d33fb73371ad7a3b6c08e5587fe9a1900eff56129d7bf8adf0b&req=dSMvEcx4n4NfWPMW1HO4ze25539ph6Zx9F5jtwYqnsTuZAe3cFPdiEd1RRjm%0A11Tl9CBkEP4r36iW79o%3D%0A)

On the [OptionFlow](https://help.bigshort.com/en/articles/10445337-looking-at-the-optionflow-tab) tab, you'll find:

  * A list of the major options trades going on in the market right now

  * The ticker, the details, the size, the direction (bullish, neutral, or bearish)

  * Across the top, you'll also see a readout of the current state of the options market.




There is simply no better way to sift through large options activity in the stock market than the BigShort OptionsFlow tab.  
â€‹  
Here you can quickly see what the big trading shops are up to, what stocks they're playing with, how aggressively, and, by clicking on the ticker, you can immediately dive deeper and identify if a trade is on.   
â€‹  
Many BigShort traders spend their day watching OptionFlow and using big moves as an impetus to trade.
