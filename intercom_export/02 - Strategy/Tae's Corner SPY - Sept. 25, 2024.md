The setup discussed below is SPY on the [Smart Flow tab](https://intercom.help/bigshort/en/articles/10445405-smartflow-tab) in BigShort and was [shared on the Discord](https://discord.com/channels/1098243246699860018/1248083166028238949/1288592393267839050) on September 25th 2024.

**Bearish Net Options Sets the Tone** : Aggressive [Net Options ](https://intercom.help/bigshort/en/articles/10354751-net-option-flow-a-complete-guide)put buying (and moderate call selling) at the open predicted a bearish turn for the day (purple oval in the image below).

**Momentum "Momo" Predicts the Move** : Bearish [Shadow Bars](https://intercom.help/bigshort/en/articles/10361455-understanding-smartflow-and-momoflow-indicators) from the retail traders ("[Momo](https://intercom.help/bigshort/en/articles/10361455-understanding-smartflow-and-momoflow-indicators)" to BigShort traders) in the first minutes of the trading day predicted an imminent ~15 minute downward thrust (red oval, below). 

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/u9e1zc58/1377741280/50f5ff4b4946b4314bd5b64ee98e/Screenshot+2025-02-12+at+2_43_33%E2%80%AFPM.png?expires=1765215000&signature=e163647d5de5fba57099c53509050bd4d31105d713e4b9b26af91756a630d25b&req=dSMgEc56nINXWfMW1HO4zRvxozbb0HEuTB6PIw8BuiRcNZ8K%2BDIOuMlhD2LQ%0AcKbweMVqL6VqGL4a9xM%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/u9e1zc58/1377741280/50f5ff4b4946b4314bd5b64ee98e/Screenshot+2025-02-12+at+2_43_33%E2%80%AFPM.png?expires=1765215000&signature=e163647d5de5fba57099c53509050bd4d31105d713e4b9b26af91756a630d25b&req=dSMgEc56nINXWfMW1HO4zRvxozbb0HEuTB6PIw8BuiRcNZ8K%2BDIOuMlhD2LQ%0AcKbweMVqL6VqGL4a9xM%3D%0A)

About 40 minutes later, a large bullish [Shadow Bar](https://intercom.help/bigshort/en/articles/10361455-understanding-smartflow-and-momoflow-indicators) predicts an immediate ~10 min bullish thrust (red box, above).

**Mid-day Bearish Signals** : In the afternoon, [Dark Pools](https://intercom.help/bigshort/en/articles/10280483-understanding-dark-pools-and-darkflow-in-bigshort) (DPs) showed up above the price and gave traders a few min to plan for a huge bearish move (blue oval, below)

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/u9e1zc58/1377750562/03c18231ba0cfa17299a86cc8fd8/Screenshot+2025-02-12+at+2_48_52%E2%80%AFPM.png?expires=1765215000&signature=6700e77d24ac9aeba7ebe1cfc546dcbc8cb6edeab86c7e458f63730f955b61e0&req=dSMgEc57nYRZW%2FMW1HO4zTshu7jXYgVPxWh13VT1o%2BUeo6VyJ5%2Ft6sypMDpa%0Ab7lRkh2MJO8bcRY7M9M%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/u9e1zc58/1377750562/03c18231ba0cfa17299a86cc8fd8/Screenshot+2025-02-12+at+2_48_52%E2%80%AFPM.png?expires=1765215000&signature=6700e77d24ac9aeba7ebe1cfc546dcbc8cb6edeab86c7e458f63730f955b61e0&req=dSMgEc57nYRZW%2FMW1HO4zTshu7jXYgVPxWh13VT1o%2BUeo6VyJ5%2Ft6sypMDpa%0Ab7lRkh2MJO8bcRY7M9M%3D%0A)

A large bearish [shadow bar](https://intercom.help/bigshort/en/articles/10361455-understanding-smartflow-and-momoflow-indicators) by the [Momentum traders](https://intercom.help/bigshort/en/articles/10361455-understanding-smartflow-and-momoflow-indicators) gave BigShort traders even more confidence before the drop (red box, above)   
​  
​**DPs Set The New Top** : Numerous and sizable Dark Pools (DPs) emerge to set the new top for the remainder of the day and suppress the price (blue oval, below).

​**Shadow Bars Predict The Rally** : In the final half hour of the day, there was a large rally perfectly predicted by the biggest set of [shadow bars](https://intercom.help/bigshort/en/articles/10361455-understanding-smartflow-and-momoflow-indicators) of the day (red box, below)
